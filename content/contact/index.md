# Contact QR Codex
We’re here to help.

If you have questions about QR codes, barcodes, scanning technologies, or any of the guides and tutorials on QR Codex, feel free to reach out. Whether you’re working on a business project, researching technology, or simply trying to understand how machine-readable codes work, we’re happy to assist.

## How to Reach Us

- Questions about QR or barcode standards
